The web is for everyone!

Responsive Design

Responsive design is all about making a website accessible on any type or size of device. The site must respond to device characteristics, such as screen size, to display a usable site. See the article on responsive design.

Accessibility

However, there is more to designing accessible sites than just responsive design (although that is a large part)! The designer must also consider users with disabilities. For example, they may use a screen reader or be dyslexic.

Check out some examples of accessibility points to consider when designing a website.
