Responsive Design and Web Accessibility

Responsive Design

Responsive design is all about making a website accessible on any type or size of device; it must respond to the device characteristics, such as screen size. See the article on responsive design.

Accessibility

However, there is more to designing sites to be accessible for everyone than just responsive design (although that makes up a large part)! The designer must also consider users accessing it under varying circumstances. For example, they may use a screen reader or be dyslexic.

Check out some examples of accessibility points to consider when designing a website.
