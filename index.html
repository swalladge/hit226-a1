Responsive Design and Web Accessibility

Responsive Design

Responsive design is all about make a website respond to different screen sizes. See the article on responsive design.

Accessibility

However, there is more to designing sites to be accessible for everyone than just responsive design! Although having a site that is viewable and runnable on any device is essential, one must also consider those accessing it under varying circumstances. For example, they may be using a screen reader, be dyslexic, or colour-blind.

Check out some examples of accessibility points to consider when designing a website.
